    About The Event

    The Machine Learning Accelerator Summit is a tiny step taken by the IEEE Student Branch, Jadavpur University to help students, engineers and tech enthusiasts live the AI dream. This is a one-day hands-on workshop cum tech talk and a lot more. We look forward to decode the most sought-after concept in today's world, down to the very fundamentals.

    Our Mission

    We are living in the world of humans and machines. It’s natural that these machines or robots have to be programmed to get them to work according to our instructions. But what if the machine started learning on its own from its experience, started to work like us, feel like us, that too without being explicitly programmed – intriguing isn’t it?

    Our Plan

    Well, we have news for you! “Think AI: The IEEE Machine Learning Summit 2019” hosted by the IEEE Student Branch of Jadavpur University brings to you a two day workshop on Machine Learning and its applications, presenting to you the opportunity of a lifetime to explore your ideas and come up with solutions to real life challenges.
